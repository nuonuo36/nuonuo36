## Theorem 

$$
\begin{align}
\frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C}
\end{align}
$$

## Proof 1

Draw a perpendicular line to create right triangles in a triangle


## Proof 2 (triangle area)


Use [**$$S_{\triangle ABC}= \frac{1}{2}ab\sin C $$**](../triangle_area)
