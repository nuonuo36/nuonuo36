## Sum 
* If $$r$$ > 1:

$$
\begin{equation}
S_n = \frac{a_1(1-r^n)}{1-r} 
\end{equation}
$$

* If -1 < $$r$$ < 1 and $$r \neq  0$$:

$$ 
\begin{equation}
S_\infty \frac{a_1}{1-r}
\end{equation}
$$


**note**: $$S_i$$ is the sum of the 1~$$i$$th term, $$a_i$$ is the $$i$$th term, $$r$$ is the common ratio, and $$n$$ is the number of terms
